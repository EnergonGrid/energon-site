ENERGON

Protocol Abstract

Energon is a live protocol operating on Flare mainnet that advances through a deterministic, height-based progression system known as Energon Height.

Protocol progression is governed by an on-chain controller that enforces strict timing, sequencing, and execution constraints. Energon Height advances only when predefined conditions are met, preserving fairness, predictability, and resistance to manipulation.

The protocol does not rely on centralized schedulers, off-chain cron jobs, or privileged operators. All state transitions are verifiable through on-chain reads and are protected by guardrails that prevent duplicate execution, race conditions, and amplification across wallets or interfaces.

Participation eligibility is enforced programmatically. Only wallets holding exactly one Energon Cube may interact with protocol mechanics. Wallets holding zero or more than one cube are excluded without exception.

Energon utilizes a native ERC-20 utility token, EON, for protocol-level mechanics and ecosystem alignment. EON does not convey ownership, equity, or claims on external assets.
